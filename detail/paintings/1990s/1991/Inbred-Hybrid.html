Notes

In Popaganda: The Art & Subversion of Ron English, this work was erroneously reported as 48 × 60 inches. The present catalogue record identifies the dimensions as 36 × 48 inches.

This work references Vincent van Gogh’s Sunflowers series (1888–1889).

This work also references Jeff Koons’s Rabbit (1986).
